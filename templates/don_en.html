💛 Support the World of Jeddi

A magical world needs light to thrive, and sometimes, a kind gesture from the heart.

This website is dedicated to preserving and sharing stories from the past. Your support helps keep these archives alive. Donations are essential to ensure the continuation and quality of our work. Many key resources—including texts accessible only through a BnF subscription, access to online databases, and digitized archives—require ongoing maintenance. In addition, there are the costs associated with proofreading and translation, carried out by specialists in Old French and the linguistic varieties from former French colonies, whose expertise is crucial for accurately reproducing period documents. Donations also enable us to purchase or rent rare or out-of-print books, essential for conducting thorough and rigorous research. Thanks to your support, we can continue to preserve, study, and make accessible a literary and historical heritage that is often difficult to access. Every contribution, however small, strengthens the sustainability of this work and directly contributes to the dissemination of invaluable knowledge.
