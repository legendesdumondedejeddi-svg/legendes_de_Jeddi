Jeddi — Keeper of Stories

⚔️ Learn all about Jeddi, the legendary hero In the vast world of Jeddi's World Legends, one name resonates with power and mystery: Jeddi. (Grandfather) A tireless researcher and guardian of ancient tales, he travels through the centuries in search of the forgotten stories that have shaped our collective imagination. Within the silent walls of the National Library of France, Jeddi delves into ancient manuscripts, deciphers words written in Old French, and breathes new life into legends passed down orally for generations. His work involves rewriting these stories in modern language, so that everyone can understand, feel, and share them once again. But his adventure doesn't end there: Jeddi also translates these stories into English, Spanish, German, and Italian, so that legends from around the world can meet and converse. He draws his inspiration from ancient books, online archives, and websites dedicated to preserving the memory of peoples, patiently weaving a bridge between the past and the present. A long-term project, undertaken with passion and respect, so that the voices of yesterday may continue to inspire those of tomorrow. Jeddi yella yeqqar-iyi-d timucuha-ya s yimi s umeẓẓuɣ A grandfather used to tell me these legends, passed down orally. Their story traverses forgotten civilizations, parallel worlds, and the whispers of the grimoire.
