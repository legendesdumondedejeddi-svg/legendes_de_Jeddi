About the Legends of Jeddi's World

Legends of the World of Jeddi is much more than an imaginary universe—it's a living world, born from passion, creativity, and the desire to tell stories together. Here, every place, every story, and every character carries a piece of soul. Legends of the World of Jeddi is a constantly evolving world, shaped by those who participate in it: writers, dreamers, explorers, and world-builders. Each contributes their stone, their breath, their light—and it is from this encounter that legends are born. 💫 Our mission is to build a space where imagination becomes a shared playground. A place where creativity is encouraged, where the community gathers around the joy of creating, where the sharing of ideas and stories unites minds. Here, creative freedom is essential: everyone can invent, dream, and forge their own path, with no limits other than those of their imagination. Whether you come to read, to write, to play, or simply to dream, Jeddi welcomes you. Here, the lines between reality and myth blur a little… And perhaps, around a bend in the path or on a page, you will discover the legend you carry within you. 🌙 Welcome to Legends of the World of Jeddi — a world to dream, to create, to experience. The legends gathered here come from ancient works, fascinating libraries, and forgotten manuscripts. We restore them and make them accessible in several languages, to offer a space where imagination connects everyone.
